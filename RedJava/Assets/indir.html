Welcome!

Welcome to pxls.space! Pxls is a multiplayer online collaborative canvas that allows you to create anything you can imagine, one pixel at a time. Join hundreds of other players in the Pxls community and create amazing works of art together as a team, or solo.

The best place to reach staff and fellow community is in the discord! There is a link below in the "links" section.

Check out our social media pages, and please take the time to read the rules below. Have fun creating (or changing) art!

Canvas Rules

We pride ourselves on trying to keep an open canvas for all free from outside interference on our end, and especially censorship. However, for the good of the community and on accounts of our own beliefs, please acknowledge and obey the following guidelines:

  1. No hateful imagery or derogatory speech. This includes but is not limited to words such as faggot, nigger, etc; as well as the Swastika, Hammer and Sickle, and any symbols of terrorism.
  2. No NSFW or NSFL content.
    • No nudity or otherwise sexually explicit content
      • No female-presenting nipples/bare breasts, genitalia, sexual fluids
    • No sexual imagery/erotica
    • No excessive blood or otherwise obscene/shocking content
  3. No more than one account per user, no exceptions. Multiple account users will be banned from creating art on the canvas.
  4. No auto-placement tools of any kind, you must place the pixels manually.
  5. Do not abuse site functionality, such as reports or lookups. (e.g. automated reporting/lookups, etc.)
  6. Any automated aggregation of data from user lookups is not allowed, and will result in a ban.
  7. Staff have final say in any rule disputes
    • If you feel a moderator has acted inappropriately, please report it to an administrator.

If you believe you have been falsely banned you may contact a moderator or administrator.

Chat Rules

  1. Keep chat civil. No harassment or homophobic/transphobic language.
  2. No hate speech. This includes emoji/symbols/ASCII art.
    • Normal swearing/etc is allowed
  3. No spamming
    • This includes excessive ASCII art/emojis/symbols/whitespace/obfuscating language
  4. No "copy pasta"s
  5. No links to sites that actively break the canvas or chat rules (e.g. porn sites)
  6. No symbology which break the canvas or chat rules (e.g. NSFW ASCII art)
  7. Staff have final say in any rule disputes
    • If you feel a moderator has acted inappropriately, please report it to an administrator.

      Help

      FAQ

      Why is the cooldown as long as it is?
      The time it takes to get a pixel is dynamic, and changes based on how many people are online. The cooldown time is longer when more users are online.
      Why does it say 0/6 pixels?
      This means that you are waiting for your next pixel. When you place a pixel, there is a cooldown for when you can place the next one. Over time, you can gain up to 6 "stacked" pixels to place at once.
      Why does it take so long to "stack" pixels?
      This is by design. It is better to place a pixel as soon as you get one. Pixel "stacking" is meant to give you a bump if you go AFK for a bit. It takes around 40 minutes to get a full 6/6 pixels, but it only takes around 3 minutes to place 6 pixels if you place them as soon as possible.
      How do I appeal a ban?
      Contact us on Discord or send an appeal to our Google form after reading the rules in the site's info panel.
      How do I make a template?
      A template is an image link that is posted in the template textbox in the settings panel so you can place over it on the canvas. You may use a regular image link to your pixel art, but many people use the 3rd party tool PxlsFiddle to turn pixel art into a fancier link. If you need to make pixel art yourself, you can use Piskel to create art to put into PxlsFiddle.
      Community members have made tutorials for these tools:Rena's Pxls templates for dummiesNetux's quick video on how to use PxlsFiddle
      How do I move a template?
      Hold CTRL (or OPTION on macOS) and click and drag to move it around. On mobile, you can tap and hold where you want to move the template and click "Move Template Here" in the pop-up.
      Does the canvas reset? When?
      Yes. The canvas resets when it is completely full. A reset date is not usually decided until the canvas is full, but the average canvas life-span is around 1 month. Updates are posted in our Discord server when a reset date is set.
      Where can I see the past canvases?
      The past canvases are featured on our Discord server, or the 3rd party website PxlsFiddle, which records timelapses and a lot of useful information on each canvas.
      How do I see who placed a pixel?
      By shift-clicking (or tap and hold on mobile) a pixel, you can see who placed a pixel, how many pixels they have placed, and when they placed it.
      How do I report someone?
      Shift-click (or tap and hold on mobile) on a pixel, and click the Report button.
      How do I change the color of my name in the chat?
      There is a "Username Color" option near the bottom of the settings panel.
      What is a "virginmap"?
      The virginmap shows which pixels have not yet been placed on (or “virgin” pixels).
      How do I report bugs?
      Submit bugs to the #dev-and-bugs channel in the Discord server. If you have found an exploit, please message one of the staff members privately (through DMs).
      How do I get more info or contact staff?
      Check the info panel (the icon on the top left) for more details and links, or join our Discord server, where a staff member will gladly respond.
